# Sequences


## Look-and-say sequence

$1,11,21,1211,111221,\cdots$

Don't think too much. Just read and count it in English.

## Fibonacci sequence

The Fibonacci sequence is given by

$$F\_0=0$$
$$F\_1=1$$
$$F\_{n+2}=F\_{n+1}+F\_n$$
